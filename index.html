AI Water Consumption

Made by Smrithika Antonette Roslyn

This demo requires a decent graphics card and up-to-date drivers.

Interactions:

  • Draw on the water to make ripples
  • Drag the background to rotate the camera
  • Press SPACEBAR to pause and unpause
  • Drag the human model to move it around
  • Press the L key to set the light direction

Aim:

  • Generative AI withdraws ~11.5–18.1 billion liters of freshwater per day (≈133k–209k L/s).
  • This simulation aims to visualize this consumption with a heightfield water simulation and real-time rendering techniques.
  • Heightfield water simulation *
  • This project was inspired by "WebGL Water" by Evan Wallace.
